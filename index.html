We help you become more profitable by outsourcing

Virtucon Business Solution is an outsourcing company that provides skilled staff offshore in Manila, Philippines so businesses from other countries can expand their workforce and shift the back-office functions overseas. Dedicated employees work exclusively for you. Due to the lower cost of labor in the Philippines, you can save between 40% and 70% on salkaries.By outsourcing your back-office work, you can focus on what you do best, making sales.

Furthermore, virtual staff can increase productivity as they can focus on the tasks they are given. So if you're looking for a Virtual Assistant, Account Manager, Customer Service, or any other back-office employee, Virtucon can help.
